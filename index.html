Funblocks Community Guidelines

Welcome to Funblocks Community:

Funblocks is a learning environment for everyone. To maintain a safe and positive space, all community members must follow these guidelines.

warning

Please pay close attention to the following guidelines.

Be Respectful
  • Treat everyone with kindness and respect
  • No bullying, harassment, or intimidation
  • No hate speech or discrimination
  • Respect different opinions and skill levels
Keep It Appropriate
  • No profanity or offensive language
  • No violent or mature content
  • No inappropriate discussions or topics
  • Keep all content suitable for all ages
Protect Privacy
  • Never share personal information
  • Do not ask others for their private details
  • No sharing of passwords or contact information
  • Report anyone asking for personal information
Be Positive
  • Encourage and support other learners
  • Give constructive feedback
  • Help keep the community friendly
  • Report inappropriate behavior to moderators
Consequences
  • First violation: Warning from moderators
  • Repeated violations: Temporary account suspension
  • Serious violations: Permanent ban from the community
  • Harassment or bullying: Immediate action taken
warning

If you see anyone doing any of the following, report it to Restudio immediately via social media.
